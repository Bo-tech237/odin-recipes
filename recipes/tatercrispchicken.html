Tater Crisp Chicken

Ingredients

  • 10 chicken legs
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups dry potato flakes

Directions

  1. Step

    2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Step

    3. Place melted butter or margarine in a shallow dish or bowl, then place potato flakes in a separate shallow dish or bowl. Roll chicken pieces in butter or margarine, then in potato flakes. Place coated chicken pieces in a 9x13 inch baking dish, skin side up.

  3. Step

    4. Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 1 hour.

Nutrition Facts
